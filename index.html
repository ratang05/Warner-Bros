THE CONJOURING

"The Conjuring" is a horror film based on the real-life investigations of paranormal experts Ed and Lorraine Warren. They are called to help a family plagued by sinister supernatural events in their farmhouse, leading to a terrifying confrontation with malevolent forces.

MARA

"Mara" is a horror thriller film about criminal psychologist Kate Fuller investigating a series of deaths linked to sleep paralysis. She discovers a malevolent entity named Mara preying on victims during their episodes, leading to a race against time to stop her.

IT

"It" is a horror novel by Stephen King about a group of children in Derry, Maine, who are terrorized by a malevolent entity that takes the form of their deepest fears, often appearing as a clown named Pennywise.

ALL OF US ARE DEAD

"All of Us Are Dead" is a South Korean television series that revolves around a high school overrun by a zombie outbreak. As chaos ensues, a group of students must band together to survive and navigate the horrors of the undead apocalypse while facing their own internal struggles and conflicts. The series explores themes of survival, friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable adversity.

By Steven Knight
